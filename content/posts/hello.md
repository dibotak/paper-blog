# Hello, World

Welcome to **Paper** — a quiet corner of the internet designed for reading, reflecting, and writing. In a world of infinite noise, we believe there is still beauty in simplicity.

## Why Paper?

Paper doesn't shout. It doesn't animate, buzz, or demand your attention. It simply holds words the way a good notebook does — faithfully, elegantly, without distraction.

This theme was built with a few principles in mind:

- **Typography is the interface.** Great reading starts with great type.
- **Speed is a feature.** Static sites load instantly, everywhere.
- **Content is king.** No sidebars. No pop-ups. Just words.

## What Comes Next

This blog is a living thing. Over time, it will fill with stories, tutorials, thoughts on technology, and perhaps a poem or two. We hope you'll return to find something worth your time.

> "The palest ink is better than the best memory."  
> — Chinese Proverb

If you're reading this, the ink has already dried. The page is turned. And the story has just begun.

Thank you for stopping by. 📝
